Our Mission

Who we are

We are passionate about Missouri and preserving the abundant resources that make it so unique. Our goal at the Magnificent Mushroom is to transform your dining experience into an adventure that will educate, inspire, and energize you unlike any other.

Our Menus are crafted around our central mission, and are designed to engage your eating experience in a unique and fulfilling way. All of Our Menus are sensitive to the environment, and will provide you with an opportunity to explore many food options.

We've been asked before how we choose Our Menu. It's simple really, we approach Our Menu as the enthusiasts we are! When we scout for local fresh produce, we choose the best produce options from the surrounding area, we ask ourselves one question, "is this something we would want to eat?" We also look very carefully at a food's potential impact to our environment. We choose Menu that are as environmentally sensitive as we are!

We've also worked very hard to make the Magnificent Mushroom more than just another restaurant. Join our community and become part of the conversation. Recommend Menu, blog about your experience in our restaurant, and share pictures and video with other team.

Did You Know?
