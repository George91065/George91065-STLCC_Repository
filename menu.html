Something for everyone

What type of Magnificent Mushroom experience do you want to have? Are you looking for an exciting vegetarian experience? A chance to relax and try new foods? As you can see, we've got you covered. We offer vegetarian menu options that are as diverse as Missouri itself! Select any of Our Menu options and learn more about pricing and availability.

If you want to refine your search a bit, feel free to browse our Featured Items menu or Wine Menu. You can even check out our cool Restaurant Blog to help you choose the foods that will give you the best experience based on your specific preferences. If you have any questions about any of our menu items, feel free to contact us, or check out our handy FAQ for a quick overview of our services.
