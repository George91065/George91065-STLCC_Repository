Come make a few friends

The Mushrooms are a community of people who love to travel, love Missouri, and want to share their journeys and experiences with others. Joining is free, so if you haven't signed up yet, do so now!

Joining up will make you eligible for discounts, extra tour goodies, exclusive side excursions, and other super-secret fun stuff! It's also a great way of making friends. We have Mushrooms-only gatherings where you can meet, mingle, and form friendships with other touring enthusisasts!

Best of all...joining is absolutely free!

So...what can you do?

When you sign up, you'll be able to write articles for our blog, submit your photos in the photo gallery, and you'll receive special members-only deals on all upcoming Magnificent Mushroom Seasonal Menus! You also get priority seating and quick check-in! Heck, if you go on enough tours, we might even have you guide a few for us!

If you're going to one of our overnight events, you'll receive a special hospitality gift when you check into your hotel. We also make sure that you're always booked for late checkout. How's that for cool?

You'll also want to check out our monthly video podcast. It includes in-depth looks at the places you can visit on Magnificent Mushroom tour showcases and occasionally features surprise guests. If our cameras catch you on a Magnificent Mushroom tour, you could be a part of it too!

One more thing...

Oh yeah, one more thing... once you join you'll receive our super-secret decoder ring (no kidding)! On many of Our Menu, your decoder ring can be used to unlock cool new trails or Magnificent Mushroom goodie packs!
