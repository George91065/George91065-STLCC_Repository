General Reservation Information

Customer notifications

When you book reservations with the Magnificent Mushroom, you should receive two notifications via email. The first will be a confirmation, which states that your reservation is booked, gives you the date and time of your reservation. The second notification should arrive on the day of your reservation. This will be a reminder notification and will contain your reservation time. If you do not receive the reminder notification, contact us immediately. We’ll make sure there are no problems in the system and confirm your reservation.

Promotional Vouchers

Some reservations include promotional vouchers. These promotional vouchers allow you to select from our featured menu or as an optional choice to replace the day’s featured items with anything of your choice. The vouchers are only good for that day and have no cash value, and cannot be redeemed if the reservation is cancelled. The promotional vouchers are negotiated with 3rd party vendors. Although Magnificent Mushroom monitors these vendors closely, we cannot guarantee that all vouchers are active.
