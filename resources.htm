Need help planning?

When you book reservations with the Magnificent Mushroom, you should receive two notifications via email. The first will be a confirmation, which states that your reservation is booked, gives you the date and time of your reservation. The second notification should arrive on the day of your reservation. This will be a reminder notification and will contain your reservation time. If you do not receive the reminder notification, contact us immediately.
